Blood Glucose Analysis - Procedures
Prepare All Procedure Specific Materials:
q Glucometer
q Test Strip
q Lancet
q 2x2 gauze pad
q Alcohol prep pad
q Bandage
Procedure:
q 1. Select appropriate site.
q 2. Blood samples for performing glucose analysis may be obtained simultaneously with intravenous access when possible .
q 3. Cleanse site appropriately with alcohol prep.
q 4. Puncture skin with lancet.
q 5. Dispose of sharps in proper container.
q 6. Wipe first drop of blood with 2x2 gauze.
q 7. Place correct amount of blood on reagent strip or site on glucometer per the manufacturers instructions.
q 8. Apply direct pressure and cover site with bandage as needed.
q 9. If result does not fit patient clinical picture:
 Consider presumptive management per Diabetic Emergencies Protocol while reassessing .
 Consider equipment error, may redraw sample and repeat analysis.
q 10. Record the time and result of the blood glucose analysis in the electronic Patient Care Report (ePCR).
