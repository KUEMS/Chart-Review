Intranasal - Procedures
Procedure:
q 1. Determine appropriate dose of medication per Protocol
q 2. Draw medication into syringe and dispose of the sharps, do not administer more than 1ml per nostril.
q 3. Attach intranasal device to syringe
q 4. With one hand, control the patient’s head
q 5. Gently introduce device into nare, stop when resistance is met.
q 6. Aim slightly upwards and toward the ear on the same side
q 7. Briskly compress the syringe to administer one half of the medication, repeat the procedure with the remaining medication on the
other nares.
 It is important for the medication to be atomized or it will not be absorbed .
q 8. Document the results in the electronic Patient Care Report (ePCR).
