Orogastric Tube Insertion - Procedures
Prepare All Procedure Specific Materials:
q Tube
q Lubricating Gel
q Securing device/Tape
q Suction
q Syringe for injecting Air
Procedure:
q 1. Measure the length of the tube from the tip of nose to earlobe to ziphoid process, mark maximum insertion depth.
q 2. Lubricate the tube with water based lubricant prior to insertion.
q 3. Insert lubricated tube through the gastric port of the BIAD or lift tongue/jaw anteriorly while passing tip lateral to endotracheal tube.
q 4. Continue to advance the tube gently until the appropriate distance is reached .
q 5. Confirm placement by injecting 20cc of air and auscultate for the whoosh or bubbling of the air over the stomach . If any doubt about
placement, remove and repeat the insertion.
q 6. Secure the tube.
q 7. Decompress the stomach by connecting the tube to low continuous suction (50-150mmHg).
q 8. Document the procedure, time, and result (success) on/with the electronic Patient Care Report (ePCR).
