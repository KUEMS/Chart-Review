# Section 163: Principles:

---

Principles:
q Apply Tourniquet as proximal as possible to wound, minimum of 2" above hemorrhage site. Do not cross joints or bony prominences with
the Tourniquet
q Secure Tourniquet in place and expedite transport.
q Document time and location of tourniquet deployment in electronic Patient Care Report (ePCR) and on device.
q Notify receiving center of tourniquet use, location of device and time placed.
q IF hemorrhage not controlled, a second tourniquet can be deployed, proximal to the first without overlap.
Procedure:
Tourniquet (CAT – Combat Application Tourniquet) -
Procedures
1. Route the self adhering band around the
extremity and pass the free-running end of the
band through the inside slit of the friction
adapter buckle
2. Pass the band through the outside slit of the
buckle, utilizing the friction adaptor buckle,
which will lock the band in place.
3. Pull the self-adhering band tight and
securely fasten the band back on itself.
4. Twist the rod until bright red bleeding has
stopped.
5. Lock the rod in place with the Windlass
Clip™
6. Hemorrhage is now controlled. Secure the
rod with the strap: Grasp the Windlass Strap™,
pull it tight and adhere it to the opposite hook
on the Windlass Clip™
