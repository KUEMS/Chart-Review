Mixing Push-Dose Epinephrine (1:100,000) -
Procedures
If 1:10,000 Pre-mixed Epi syringe is Available:
q 1. Waste 9mL from a pre-mixed syringe of Epinephrine 1:10,000 (leaving 1mL in the syringe)
q 2. Draw 9mL of Normal Saline back inot the ampule (1mL Epinephrine + 9mL Normal Saline)
 Resulting Solution Concentration: 10mcg Epi per mL (1:100,000 Epi)
q 5. Label the syringe to visually distinguish this syringe as the push-dose concentration

P Paramedic
Equipment Needed if 1:10,000 Pre-mixed Epi syringe is NOT Available:
q 1. Expel 1mL of saline from a 0.9% Normal Saline flush, leaving 9mL remaining in the syringe
q 2. Draw up 1mL of 1:1,000 Epinephrine from vial
q 3. Expel 9mL of the solution, leaving only 1mL remaining in the syringe
q 4. Draw up 9mL of Normal Saline, filling the syringe to 10mL.
 Resulting Solution Concentration: 10mcg Epi per mL (1:100,000 Epi)
q 5. Label the syringe to visually distinguish this syringe as the push-dose concentration.
